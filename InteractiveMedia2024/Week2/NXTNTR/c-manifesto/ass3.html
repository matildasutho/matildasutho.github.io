COWGIRLS





aka Punk People, aka the antidote to Big Daddy Data Wranglers















When we are encouraged to detach from ourselves and the world around us,
it feels punk to resist that in favour of something else, something intertwined
with the flow of the river,
the mourning of trees,
and our connection to other beings
